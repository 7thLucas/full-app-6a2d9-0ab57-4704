# Product Overview — AviSignal

## What It Is
AviSignal is an Android app that helps a team track, log, and analyse Aviator game signals using AI-driven pattern analysis and historical data. It surfaces current and forward-looking trend insights, maintains a full signal history, and scores prediction accuracy so the team can continuously sharpen its strategy.

## Users & Audience
- **Primary users**: Internal team members who follow and act on Aviator signals together.
- **Use context**: Shared team signal coordination — issuing calls, reviewing history, measuring who and what is accurate.

## Positioning
A private team analytics tool. Not a public consumer product. The game runs on a provably fair RNG so no signal is a guarantee, but AviSignal replaces guesswork and scattered chat with a disciplined tracking layer: every signal is logged, every outcome recorded, every accuracy score visible to the whole team.

## Core Features
1. **AI Signal Display** — Live signal recommendations derived from pattern analysis of historical round data.
2. **Signal History Log** — Persistent record of every signal with timestamps, round outcomes, and result status.
3. **Accuracy Metrics** — Per-signal and aggregate accuracy scores; team performance visible over time.
4. **Trend Visualisation** — Charts showing current and projected patterns derived from rolling historical data windows.
5. **Team-Shared Feed** — A single shared signal feed accessible in real-time by all team members on Android.
6. **Future Trend Forecast** — Forward-looking pattern projections to inform upcoming signal decisions.

## Brand & Tone
Clean, data-first, confident. Professional internal tool aesthetic — dark analytical panels, amber/gold accents, clear readouts. Not a flashy consumer product.

## Platform
Android (native mobile app).

## Strategic Principles
- **Honesty over hype**: position as a pattern-tracking and accuracy-monitoring tool, not a guaranteed predictor.
- **Team transparency**: all signals, outcomes, and accuracy scores visible to every team member.
- **Data discipline**: every signal logged, every outcome captured, nothing disappears into a chat thread.
- **Continuous improvement**: accuracy metrics accumulate over time and drive strategy refinement.

## Scope (MVP)
- AI signal display (pattern-based, from historical data)
- Signal history list with outcomes and result status
- Accuracy metrics dashboard (per-signal + aggregate)
- Basic trend chart (current + forward projection)
- Team-shared signal feed on Android
